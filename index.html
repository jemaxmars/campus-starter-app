Frequently Asked Questions

What are the hours of the library?

The Orion Library is open 24 hours from Sunday at noon until Friday at 8 PM. It is open from 10 AM to 6 PM on Saturdays.

Where can I get technical help?

The IT Help Desk is located on the first floor of the Orion Library. You can also submit a ticket online through the university portal.

How do I join a student club?

Visit the Student Activities Fair at the beginning of each semester, or browse the list of clubs on the Student Union website and contact the club leaders directly.
